Do’s & Don’ts for a Perfect Marriage Biodata

Making a good first impression with your marriage biodata is your chance to shine. Here are some crucial points to bear in mind:

Do’s

  • Highlight your strengths—education, job, interests, and personality.
  • Use a polite and professional tone.
  • Proofread to avoid any typos or errors.
  • Pick a clean, attractive format (like those on MyShadiBiodata).
  • Add a high-quality, smiling photo.

Don’ts

  • Don’t lie or exaggerate—honesty builds trust.
  • Avoid negative talk or past relationship details.
  • Don’t overshare—keep it first-meeting appropriate.
  • Skip generic templates—make it personal and unique.
  • Don’t delay—prepare early and review before sharing.
